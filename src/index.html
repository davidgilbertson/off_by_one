Clue

How it works

You will be given a clue, like 'friendly peacock'.

The answer is a pair of words that are the same, except for one letter (they're off by one).

Naturally, the two words are the same length.

You can get a series of hints by clicking the 'Hint' button.

For example, the answer to the above clue is made of words with 8 letters.

The first letter is 'P', the second is different, and so on.

The answer in this case is 'pleasant pheasant'.
